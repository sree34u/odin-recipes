Witch’s Brew Tea

Ingredients

To conjure this magical delicious Witch’s Brew Tea , gather the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup (240 ml) moon-blessed water
  • 1 black demon tea bag (or 1 tsp loose demon tea)
  • A drizzle of forest nectar (honey) or angel's tears (optional)
  • A splash of cloud milk or a slice of sun lemon (optional)

Spell Instructions

Follow these sacred steps to summon your tea potion:
  1. Boil the Moon Water: Heat the water until it reaches a rolling, bubbling boil.
  2. Summon the Tea Spirit: Place your tea bag or loose leaf in an infuser inside your cauldron (a cup will do).
  3. Pour with Purpose: Gently pour the boiling water over the tea vessel to awaken its essence.
  4. Steep the Magic: Let it steep for 3 to 5 minutes—longer for stronger powers.
  5. Release the Leaves: Remove the tea bag or infuser carefully—do not anger the herbs.
  6. Sweeten the Spell: Add sugar, honey, milk, or lemon according to your potion's alignment.
  7. Stir the Brew: Stir clockwise while whispering your intentions into the steam.
  8. Consume with Care: Sip slowly, and let the enchantment unfold within you.
Your mystical **Witch Tea** is ready. Drink by candlelight or under moonlight for full effect.
